From top left to bottom right:

- <b>Google Greater Mountain View Campus</b> - With the Mountain View, Moffied Field
  and Sunnyvale campuses combined, it houses 20k+ Googlers combined. Due to its unique
  architecture and perks, it definetly feels more like a very large university campus
  than a workspace!
- <b>Round1 Amusement Arcade</b> - Large chain arcade originating from Japan. Great
  place to destress!
- <b>Anime Expo</b> - North America's largest Anime convention!{" "}
- <b>Balboa Park</b> - Park located in San Diego with an artist village, museums,
  and gardens!
- <b>El Palo Alto</b> - Redwood/Sequoia trees are sort of everywhere in South Bay,
  and they're very tall! This one is notable for being the namesake of the city Palo
  Alto, at least according to Wikipedia.
- <b>In 'N Out</b> - Very solid burger joint that's almost exclusively in California.
- <b>RSA Conference</b> - Cybersecurity conference held in San Francisco.
- <b>Venice Beach</b> - Really nice beach in Los Angeles. Tried surfing here, it
  did not go well!{" "}
- <b>Waymo</b> - Self driving car service that uses LIDAR technology. Very smooth!
