This is the Smokin R&R's food truck in Stittsville, Ontario (it's near Kanata South).
I would consider them to be \#2 on best places to eat ever, and I usually get the 
pulled pork poutine. Good for an entire day, like any poutine to be honest, but it 
quite literally is the golden standard for poutine. If only they had similar in the
Bay Area :(

Sadly this truck is elsewhere half the time and is only open during the summers, 
so when I originally posted this, it was closed. This is just the reply post :D
